# Logging In

Learn how to access your Kadouri CRM account securely.

## Accessing Kadouri CRM

Visit your company's Kadouri CRM URL (provided by your administrator) and log in with your credentials.

### First-Time Login

If this is your first time logging in:

1. Check your email for an invitation from Kadouri CRM
2. Click the invitation link
3. Set your password
4. Enable two-factor authentication (recommended)
5. Complete your profile

### Standard Login

1. Visit the Kadouri CRM URL
2. Enter your email address
3. Enter your password
4. Click "Sign In"

## Forgot Password

If you forgot your password:

1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Click the reset link
5. Choose a new password

## Security Features

- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**: Add extra security with MFA
- **Session Timeout**: Automatic logout after inactivity
- **Secure Connection**: All data encrypted with HTTPS

## Troubleshooting

**Can't remember my email**: Contact your system administrator

**Not receiving reset email**: Check spam folder or contact support

**Account locked**: Contact your administrator to unlock

## Next Steps

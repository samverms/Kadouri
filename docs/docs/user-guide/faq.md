# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Common questions and answers about using Kadouri CRM.

## General Questions

### What is Kadouri CRM?

Kadouri CRM is an enterprise order management system designed specifically for agricultural and produce sales. It helps you manage customers, create orders, track commissions, and sync everything with QuickBooks Online.

### How do I log in?

Visit your company's Kadouri CRM URL and sign in with your email and password. If you don't have login credentials, contact your system administrator.

### I forgot my password. What do I do?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

### What browsers are supported?

Kadouri CRM works best on modern browsers:
- Google Chrome (recommended)
- Microsoft Edge
- Firefox
- Safari

## Accounts & Customers

### How do I add a new customer?

1. Click "Accounts" in the navigation menu
2. Click the "+ New Account" button
3. Fill in the customer details (name, type, contact info)
4. Add addresses and contacts as needed
5. Click "Save"

### What's the difference between a Seller and a Buyer?

- **Seller**: Someone who sells products to you (suppliers, vendors)
- **Buyer**: Someone who buys products from you (customers, clients)

### Can I add multiple addresses for one customer?

Yes! When editing an account, you can add as many addresses and contacts as needed.

### How do I search for a customer?

Use the search bar at the top of the page or the search field in the Accounts list to find customers by name, email, or phone number.

## Orders

### How do I create a new order?

1. Go to "Orders" → "+ New Order"
2. Select the seller (who you're buying from)
3. Select the buyer (who you're selling to)
4. Add order lines (products, quantities, prices)
5. Commissions are calculated automatically
6. Click "Save"

### Can I edit an order after creating it?

Yes, you can edit orders that are in "Draft" or "Confirmed" status. Once an order is posted to QuickBooks, editing is limited.

### How are commissions calculated?

Commissions are calculated automatically based on the commission rate set for each product line. The system calculates: `Commission = Line Total × Commission Rate`

### What do the order statuses mean?

- **Draft**: Order is being created, not finalized
- **Confirmed**: Order is confirmed but not yet in QuickBooks
- **Posted to QB**: Order has been synced to QuickBooks as an invoice
- **Paid**: Payment has been received (from QuickBooks)

### How do I generate a PDF for an order?

Open the order and click the "Generate PDF" button. You can create separate PDFs for the seller and buyer, or generate both at once.

### What reports are available?

Common reports include:

Yes! Most reports have an "Export" button that downloads the data as an Excel file.

### How do I create a custom report?

> "Create a report of top 10 customers by revenue"

## QuickBooks Integration

### What does QuickBooks sync do?

The QuickBooks integration automatically:
- Creates customers in QuickBooks from your accounts
- Creates items in QuickBooks from your products
- Creates invoices in QuickBooks from your orders
- Updates payment status when invoices are paid

### How often does it sync?

- Manual sync: Click "Sync to QuickBooks" on any order
- Automatic sync: Happens when you post an order
- Payment status: Updates via webhooks in real-time

### What if a QuickBooks sync fails?

Check the error message for details. Common issues:
- Duplicate customer names
- Missing required fields
- QuickBooks connection expired (need to re-authenticate)

### Can I disconnect from QuickBooks?

Contact your system administrator to disconnect the QuickBooks integration.

## Permissions & Access

### Why can't I see certain features?

Your role determines what features you can access. This is by design to keep the interface simple and focused on your job responsibilities.

### How do I request additional permissions?

Contact your system administrator or manager to discuss your access needs.

### What can each role do?

**Admin**: Everything
**Sales**: Accounts, Orders, Products, Reports
**Back Office**: Accounts, Orders, Contracts, Email
**Accountant**: Invoices, Reports (view-only for orders)

## Technical Issues

### The page isn't loading. What should I do?

1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your internet connection
5. Contact support if the issue persists

### I'm seeing an error message. What does it mean?

Take a screenshot of the error and contact support. Include:
- What you were trying to do
- The exact error message
- Your username (not password!)

### Can I use Kadouri CRM on my phone?

Yes! The interface is responsive and works on mobile devices, though some features work better on a computer.

### How do I report a bug?

Contact support with:
- Description of the problem
- Steps to reproduce it
- Screenshots if possible
- Your browser and device info

## AI Chat Assistant

### What can the AI assistant help with?

The AI assistant can:
- Answer questions about how to use features
- Look up account and order information
- Create custom reports
- Guide you through complex tasks
- Troubleshoot common issues

### How do I ask the AI a question?

Click the chat icon and type your question in plain English. Examples:

> "How do I add a customer?"

> "Show me orders from ABC Produce"

> "Create a sales report for this month"

### What if the AI doesn't understand my question?

Try rephrasing it or being more specific. You can also:
- Break complex questions into smaller parts
- Use specific names and dates
- Ask follow-up questions to clarify

### Is my data safe when using the AI?

Yes! The AI only has access to data you're permitted to see based on your role. All conversations are secure and private.
